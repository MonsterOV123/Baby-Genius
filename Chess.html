How Puzzle Mode Works

  • Some puzzles are checkmate in 1.
  • Some puzzles ask for a strong move like a check, capture, or fork.
  • Click a white piece, then click where it should go.
  • Or drag the piece to the correct square.

Kid-Friendly Tip

Pawns move forward. They usually go 1 square, but on their first move they can go 2 squares.

How To Play

  • Click one of your pieces.
  • Then click a highlighted destination square.
  • You can play as White or Black.
  • The computer will answer after your move.
